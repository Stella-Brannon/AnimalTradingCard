Great Gray Owl

This owl species is known for its silent flight, which allows it to sneak up on prey without being heard.

  • Scientific Name: Strix nebulosa
  • Average Height: 60 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: Meadows and wetlands

The Great Gray Owl is a large owl species that is native to the northern hemisphere, particularly in North America. It is known for its distinctive facial disc, which is a circular pattern of feathers around its eyes that helps to focus sound waves towards its ears, allowing it to locate prey with great accuracy.
